What is Academic Hub?

Academic Hub is a digital platform designed to streamline academic management for both students and faculty. It helps in tracking attendance and marks making the learning process seamless and efficient.

With Academic Hub, students can access their academic records anytime, and faculty members can update information effortlessly. The goal is to enhance productivity and create a well-organized learning environment.
